BestCheckout Help Center

Get your checkout ready to sell.

Plain-language guidance for connecting your Shopify store, setting up payments, and launching a purchase flow.

Connect your Shopify store

Create an app in your own Shopify Dev Dashboard, then connect it to BestCheckout with its Client ID and Client secret.

Your app stays under your controlBestCheckout does not create or own the Shopify app. You can revoke access whenever you need.
1

Open App development in Shopify

In Shopify admin, go to Settings → Apps → App development. If Shopify shows Build apps in Dev Dashboard, choose it to continue.

2

Create an app in Dev Dashboard

Open Apps, choose Create app, and name it clearly, for example “BestCheckout connection”. Save the basic app information.

3

Copy the Client ID and Client secret

Open the new app, choose Settings, and find the Credentials section. Copy both values. Keep the Client secret private; BestCheckout will not show it again after connection.

4

Return to BestCheckout and connect

Enter the myshopify.com store address, Client ID, and Client secret. Then choose Connect store. We verify the connection before any checkout goes live.
